DataSHIELD v6.1.1

The last release of DataSHIELD was a minor (x.x.X) change, including multiple bugfixes as flagged up by users.

If you have found a bug that you would like to see fixed in DataSHIELD, we would love to hear about it. Please post on our DataSHIELD forums (creating an account if you don't already have one is quick and easy) with as many details as you can- this will be picked up by our team and worked into a future release!

 

DataSHIELD v6.2

This planned release of DataSHIELD will be a major (x.X.x) change, bringing new functions to analyse within DataSHIELD.

New methods:
  • ds.dmtC2S
  • ds.forestplot
  • ds.metadata
Improved methods:
  • ds.replaceNA
  • ds.tapply.assign
  • ds.lexis
  • ds.dataFrameFill
  • ds.mean
  • ds.var
  • ds.table

Function Requests

Do you have a burning desire to see a statistical analysis technique implemented into DataSHIELD for your own use?

It may be that others would like to see it too. The best way to make it visible to us is to post on our DataSHIELD forums (creating an account if you don't already have one is quick and easy). We will then be able to tell you at what stage in the future it may be practical to implement it. We would also encourage supporting you develop new DataSHIELD functionality...
